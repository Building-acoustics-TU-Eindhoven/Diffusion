Calculation of reverberation time from a Schroeder decay (Schroeder 1965)

Parameters:

  • t (array of floats) – Time array of time steps

  • sch_db (array) – Energy density over time after the source is switched off at the receiver position

  • idx_w_rec (int) – Time index at which the source is switched off

t60 – Reverberation time T_{60}
